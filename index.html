About

My interest in programming started in middle school math. They had a teacher from the local high school talk to the accelerated classes about programming and how it is used. I remember looking at my friend with such amazement and it was decided.

I began with some web design and c++ freshman year of high school but that ended abrubtly after an accident which resulted in missing most of my freshman year. My love for video games brought it back into my life when I would try and change mods in games like Minecraft. Once my time at a junior college started, I began taking every programming class I could (within the degree requirements). I took PASCAL, C, C++, Java, and a data structures course. But the only course that used real world scenarios was the latter. That's when I decided to start teaching myself after I finished my associates in general education, to challenge my productivity and learn things that wouldn't be in the class curriculum.

This leads us to today, where I am currently learning HTML, CSS, SASS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Jquery and more! I quickly recieved my certificate of completion for Free Code Camp's front end development courses and am now honing my skills before learning more.
